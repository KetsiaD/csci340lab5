  • Blog Idea

    I have been wanting to create my own blog of a long time, but I did not have th skills it take to create one and I had no idea of what my content will be. During my Databases and Websystems class, we were tasked to create a blog as part of weekly assignments for the class. Well this time I had no excuse not to create one, afterall, I don't want to fail the class.

  • Created my First Blog

    Well, I had to learn programing languages that I will be using on creating my blog, and I finally created an empty blog, but it is now showing live on my webpage. yaayy!!!!

  • Finding the Right Content

    I always thought creating a blog, that includes learning the programing languages I need to create one was going to be the hardest part. But that is not true. Finding the right content that reflects what I actually want my audience to know about me, my interests, and my business(still in mind) is very challenging. There are alot about a human being and what they go through on a daily basis, but out of all that, what is worth sharing? What would help people? All these are questions I aksed myself to get an idea of what my blog would be about.

  • Now Blogging...

    After spending hours and days with a blank blog, I just decided, what to me was so basic, writing about my daily reflections. This was just like a place holder for my blog content, I was planning on adding more to it whenever I get an idea. I started writing my First reflection. If I tell you, I have never had a very relaxing, self reflection time as good as the one I had writing about my first post. I relieved my whole day, analyzing every situation, encouter, drama,conversation I had with people. I understood and noticed alot of patterns in how I repsond and interact with people. Writing my daily reflections, daily lifestyle has been a moment for self discovery.
